Frequently Asked Questions

Isn't dangerous to run while playing Chase?

No, because your safety is our priority. Chase will organize chases in parks, gardens and safe areas to understand and analyze behaviors. We will expand areas to cities and develop audio features and a stand-alone app for watches for a better experience.

Can I play with my connected watch?

For the V1, we won't support yet watches, but we'll work on it for the next versions. We understand how important it is for the runners

Is Chase already available?

Not yet, but a public version is scheduled for Q1 2020.

What are the rewards in the game ?

You can find game custom skins and collectibles; but also gift cards, products from companies listed above. For the next versions, we will launch "Crypto Chase", players will have the opportunity to earn cryptocurrencies during chases.

Do Chase will have augmented reality content?

Yes. In the future, you will have to discover/find hidden objects with AR.

Is Chase similar to Pokemon go?

Chase is inspired by Pokemon Go & League of legends, instead of capturing pokemons, You run to capture the prize and protect it from the others. Chase is player-centric and empowers interaction between players.
